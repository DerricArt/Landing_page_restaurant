About Us

Established in 2025, Mangan Tamo began as a part-time online business. "Mangan Tamo" is a Pangasinan term meaning "Let's Eat", reflecting our warm invitation to everyone to experience the joy of Filipino dining.

Our mission is simple: to serve home-cooked style dishes that remind you of family gatherings, childhood memories, and the comfort of authentic Filipino cuisine. Every recipe is crafted with love, using traditional techniques passed down through generations.
